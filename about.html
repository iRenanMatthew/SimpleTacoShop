Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Jeffrey M. Pilcher, taco historian and professor of history at the Universty of Minnestoa, says the earliest mention of tacos in the United States are in a newspaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
